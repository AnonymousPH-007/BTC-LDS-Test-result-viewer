To all the passers, please take note of the following list of requirements to apply for your Radio Operator Certificate (RSL) and Radio Station License (RSL):

Requirements for Amateur Radio Operator Certificate/Amateur Radio Station License

  • 1. Duly Accomplished Application Form: APPLICATION FOR AMATEUR RADIO OPERATOR CERTIFICATE/AMATEUR RADIO STATION LICENSE [Form No. BTC1-03]
  • 2. Copy of Valid Report of Rating
  • 3. Copy of Permit to Purchase /Possess
  • 4. Copy of AT-ROC
  • 5. Copy of Document Indicating Source of Equipment:
    • * For locally sourced equipment: Official Receipt or Sales Invoice from authorized Radio Dealer.
    • * For imported equipment: Copy of Invoice from the supplier AND Copy of Permit to Import.
    • * For equipment from licensed Amateur: Duly accomplished APPLICATION FOR PERMIT TO PURCHASE/POSSESS/SELL/TRANSFER [Form No. BTC1-09] AND Original AT-RSL of the Seller.
      Note: Apply for Duplicate Copy if Original is lost/mutilated/destroyed or not available.
